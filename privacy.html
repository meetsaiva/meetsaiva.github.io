SAIVA Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

At SAIVA, we believe privacy is sacred. We are committed to protecting your personal data with the same care and integrity we bring to every part of the SAIVA experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Who We Are

Two Wings, Inc. ("Two Wings," "we," "us," or "our") is a wellness technology company that offers AI-guided tools for personal reflection, microdosing support, and intentional self-inquiry. Our services are intended for educational and personal growth purposes only.

2. What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The SAIVA website, mobile interface, and software
  • All usage during our Proof-of-Concept (POC) phase
  • Data collected from any features, prompts, or tools associated with your SAIVA experience

This policy does not apply to third-party websites or services we may link to.

3. What We Collect

We collect two types of information:

A. Personal Information (you provide directly)

Examples may include:

  • Your name or email (if you opt-in or create an account)
  • Responses to onboarding or intake questions
  • Consent acknowledgments
  • Feedback or contact form submissions
Note: You are not required to submit personal information to use the POC version of SAIVA. Anonymous use is supported wherever possible.

B. Usage Data (collected automatically)

This includes:

  • Session start and end times
  • AI interaction patterns (e.g., prompt types used, frequency)
  • Device and browser type
  • General location (IP-based, non-identifiable)
  • Consent timestamps (e.g., checkbox agreements)

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the SAIVA experience
  • Enhance safety and reflection quality
  • Monitor platform performance and fix bugs
  • Understand general user behavior (anonymized analytics)
  • Respond to inquiries or support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising or retargeting.

5. How We Protect Your Data

We use industry-standard security practices, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Firewalled servers with limited access
  • Role-based internal access controls
  • Anonymization of AI training data where applicable
Your session data is never sold. Ever.

6. When We Share Data (and When We Don't)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We may share data only when:

  • Required by law, subpoena, or legal process
  • Needed to protect user safety or the integrity of the platform
  • Working with trusted service providers who help us run SAIVA (under confidentiality agreements)

If SAIVA is ever acquired, merged, or transferred, your data will be handled with equal or greater protection, and you will be notified.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, email: timsaekoo@gmail.com

8. Data Retention

We retain anonymized usage data for as long as necessary to:

  • Improve platform safety
  • Develop our AI systems responsibly
  • Comply with regulatory and legal obligations

If you submit personal information (e.g., name or email), you may request deletion at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

SAIVA is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Users

SAIVA is based in the United States. If you access SAIVA from outside the U.S., you consent to the transfer, storage, and processing of your information in the U.S. under U.S. privacy laws, which may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and, if the changes are material, provide additional notice (such as via a banner or email).
